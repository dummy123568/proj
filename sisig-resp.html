Pork Sisig

  • 12 tbsp Vinegar
  • 18 cloves Garlic
  • 6 pcs Bay leaves
  • 3 tsp Black peppercorns

Instructions

  1. 1Boil pork belly until tender, about 30 minutes, then drain
  2. 2Grill or broil pork until crispy, then chop into small pieces
  3. 3Heat butter in a sizzling plate or pan
  4. 4Sauté chopped onions and chili peppers
  5. 5Add chopped pork and mix well
  6. 6Add soy sauce and calamansi juice, stir for 2 minutes
  7. 7Mix in mayonnaise and top with a raw egg
  8. 8Serve sizzling hot with rice
