Giniling

⏱️ 35 minutes
  • • 225g 450g 675g ground pork or beef
  • • 1 small 1 medium 1 large onion, chopped
  • • 2 cloves 3-4 cloves 5-6 cloves garlic, minced
  • • 1/2 medium 1 medium 1 large potato, peeled and diced
  • • 1/2 medium 1 medium 1 large carrot, peeled and diced
  • • 1/2 cup 1 cup 1 ½ cups tomato sauce
  • • 1 tbsp 2 tbsp 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • • 1/2 tbsp 1 tbsp 1 ½ tbsp fish sauce (patis)
  • • 1/4 cup 1/2 cup 3/4 cup frozen green peas
  • • 1 tbsp 1-2 tbsp 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • • to taste to taste to taste salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. 1Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté chopped onions until softened and translucent.
  2. 2Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. 3Add ground pork or beef. Cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned and crumbly. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  4. 4Stir in diced carrots and potatoes, mixing well with the meat.
  5. 5Pour in tomato sauce, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Stir to combine.
  6. 6Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until vegetables are tender. Stir occasionally.
  7. 7Add frozen green peas and cook for another 2-3 minutes until tender-crisp.
  8. 8Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Adjust any seasonings as desired.
  9. 9Serve hot with steamed white rice.
