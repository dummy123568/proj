Sinigang na Baboy

⏱️ 40 minutes
    • • 1/2 kg 1 kg 1.5 kg pork (or shrimp/fish)
    • • 4 cups 7 cups 10 cups water
    • • 1 small 2 medium 3 large tomatoes
    • • 1 small 1 medium 2 medium onion
    • • 1 pack 2 packs 3 packs sinigang mix (or tamarind)
    • • 1 2 3 radish (labanos)
    • • 1 2 3 eggplant
    • • 5 pcs 10 pcs 15 pcs string beans (sitaw)
    • • 1 bunch 2 bunches 3 bunches kangkong
    • • 1–2 pcs 2–3 pcs 3–4 pcs green chili
    • • to taste to taste to taste fish sauce (patis)
    1. 1Boil water with pork until tender.
    2. 2Add tomatoes and onion.
    3. 3Add sinigang mix or tamarind.
    4. 4Put radish, eggplant, and sitaw.
    5. 5Add chili and simmer.
    6. 6Add kangkong last.
    7. 7Season with fish sauce and serve.
