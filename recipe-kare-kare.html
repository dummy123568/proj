Kare-Kare

⏱️ 1 hour and 30 minutes
  • • 1/2 kg 1 kg 1.5 kg oxtail or beef
  • • 3 cups 6 cups 9 cups water
  • • 2 tbsp 4 tbsp 6 tbsp peanut butter
  • • 1 tbsp 2 tbsp 3 tbsp ground rice (or rice flour)
  • • 1 2 3 eggplant (sliced)
  • • 5 pcs 10 pcs 15 pcs string beans (sitaw)
  • • 1 bunch 2 bunches 3 bunches pechay or bok choy
  • • 2 cloves 4 cloves 6 cloves garlic
  • • 1 2 3 onion
  • • 1 tbsp 2 tbsp 3 tbsp annatto powder (atsuete)
  • • to taste to taste to taste salt and pepper
  • • 2 tbsp 4 tbsp 6 tbsp bagoong (for serving)

Instructions

  1. 1Boil oxtail or beef in water until tender (about 1–2 hours)
  2. 2Set aside the broth and meat
  3. 3Sauté garlic and onion in a pan
  4. 4Add peanut butter and some broth, then mix well
  5. 5Add ground rice to thicken the sauce
  6. 6Add the cooked meat and simmer for a few minutes
  7. 7Add vegetables like eggplant, sitaw, and pechay
  8. 8Add annatto for color, season with salt and pepper
  9. 9Serve hot with bagoong on the side
