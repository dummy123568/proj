Chicken Adobo

⏱️ 45 minutes
  • • 500g 1000g 1500g chiken thighs
  • • 4 tbsp 8 tbsp 12 tbsp Soy sauce
  • •4 tbsp 8 tbsp 12 tbsp vinigar
  • • 1 2 3 Soy sauce
  • • 6 12 18 garlic cloves
  • • 2 pcs 4 pcs 6 pcs Bay leaves
  • • 1 tsp 2 tsp 3 tsp Black peppercorns
  • • 200 ml 400 ml 600 ml Water
  • • 2 tbsp 4 tbsp 6 tbsp cooking oil

    • Instructions

    1. 1Marinate chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, crushed garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns for 30 minutes
    2. 2Heat oil in a pan and sauté the marinated chicken until lightly browned
    3. 3Pour in the marinade and add water
    4. 4Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-35 minutes
    5. 5Turn chicken pieces occasionally until tender and sauce thickens
    6. 6Adjust seasoning if needed with more soy sauce or vinegar
    7. 7Remove bay leaves before serving
    8. 8Serve hot with steamed rice
