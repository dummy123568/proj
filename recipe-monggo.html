Ginisang Munggo (Monggo)

⏱️ 60 minutes
  • • 1/2 cup 1 cup 1 1/2 cups munggo beans
  • • 1–2 tbsp 2–3 tbsp 3–4 tbsp cooking oil
  • • 2 cloves 4 cloves 6 cloves garlic (minced)
  • • 1 small 2 small 3 small onion (chopped)
  • • 1 small 2 small 3 small tomato (chopped)
  • • 1/4 cup 1/2 cup 3/4 cup pork (optional) or dried fish (optional)
  • • 3–4 cups 6–7 cups 8–10 cups water
  • • 1 cup 2 cups 3 cups malunggay leaves (optional)
  • • to taste to taste to taste salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. 1Boil munggo beans in water until soft (around 30–45 minutes).
  2. 2In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté garlic, onion, and tomato until aromatic.
  3. 3Add pork or dried fish (if using) and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. 4Pour in the boiled munggo along with its water.
  5. 5Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 5–10 minutes to blend flavors.
  6. 6Add malunggay leaves and cook for another minute.
  7. 7Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve hot.
